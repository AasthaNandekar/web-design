Tribute to Major Sandeep Unnikrishnan

Major Sandeep Unnikrishnan was a brave officer of the National Security Guard of India. He sacrificed his life while fighting terrorists during the 26/11 Mumbai attacks. His courage, leadership, and dedication to the nation made him a true hero.

He was awarded the Ashoka Chakra, India's highest peacetime gallantry award. Major Sandeep Unnikrishnan will always be remembered for his bravery and patriotism.
