The Indian Air Force is responsible for protecting the airspace of India.



History of Indian Air Force

The Indian Air Force was established on 8 October 1932 during British rule. After independence in 1947, it became one of the strongest air forces in the world and has played an important role in many wars and humanitarian missions.
