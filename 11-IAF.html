Welcome to Indian Air Force Website

The Indian Air Force is the air arm of the Indian Armed Forces responsible for protecting the airspace of India.



History of Indian Air Force

The Indian Air Force was established on 8 October 1932 during British rule. After India gained independence in 1947, it became an important part of national defense. The Air Force has participated in several wars and humanitarian missions including the 1965 and 1971 wars and disaster relief operations.
